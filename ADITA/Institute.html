About This Intitute
West Bengal is on the threshold of enteruing an
ear of industrial rejuvenation thanks to the new industrial policy of the Govt. Of West Bengal. Atthis juncture there will be an increasing demand of trained rechnical person capable of driving our state in the 21st Century.Day by day the industries are orienteing themselves towards automation & Computerisation resulting in a huge demand of qualitified Computer professional. Private computer tranning institute are offering various course to take advantage of this situation where Govt. intitutes are not enough in number to cater to this demand of computer traning.

The Sports & Youth Services Depertment(Youth
Service Branch) of Govt. of West Bengal has
come forward in this connection to help set up Computer Tranning Institute to cater to the increasing needs of the Youth of West Bengal.
Being encouraged by the success achieved by the Computer Tranning Intitute, State Youth Center Kolkata-the facilities of Computer Tranning are gradually being expanded to inclued the District & sub-divisional headquarters of West Bengal & also the rural areas.
