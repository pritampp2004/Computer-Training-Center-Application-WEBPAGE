Rules & regulation For The Students
The student applyimg for addmission must read the prospectus carefully & convince
himself that he qualifies for the traning. In case, if in any part of the admission
process or after the commencement of classes he iss found to be ineligible, he may
expelled from the institute & no refound will be given in that case.
 Students applying for the One year Diploma course may take note of the fact that,
unless they obtain a minimum of 50% marks in the 1st Semester, they will be
allowed to take the 2nd Semestar of the course. However, if they got 40-49%
marks, they will be awarded the Corresponding Six months course's certificate.
 Student appling for the One year Diploma Course will not be allowed to shorten
his course to the six-month certificate course, unless he has been disqualified for the
2nd Semestar.
